What is Mental Health

It can even affect a person physically like making a person feel as if they have less energy than before or having trouble sleeping. Mental health is often overlooked in society. This is a problem not many people are aware of. Common mental illnesses such as depression and anxiety often go ignored and can lead to severe consequences.

Overlooking Mental Health is a Problem

Socially, mental health is believed to not be as important as physical health which is the main reason it is overlooked so often. While physical health is important, mental health is just as important. Just like being worried over a sprained ankle, you should be worried if you see someone acting more depressed than usual. Ignoring how someone is feeling mentally can lead to terrible consequences, being aware and showing that you care can mean so much to people who are struggling.
