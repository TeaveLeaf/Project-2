Being Aware

Signs and How to Help

Possible Signs of Deppression

Less energy than usual Loss of interest in usual activities Feeling anxious and restless Changes in sleeping pattern Isolating oneself Irritability Feelings of lonliness

How you can help

There are so many different ways you can help and show support to those who are struggling with poor mental health. If someone you care about is showing constant signs of poor mental health having a conversation with them can be helpful. Showing unconditional support and having an open-mind while talking to them can mean so much. Getting help and setting an appointment with a medical professional can be the first steps of getting help to your loved one. Showing you care can save someones life. Ignoring the signs can lead to irreversible changes. Spreading awareness and getting rid of the idea that mental health is a taboo topic can help so many people who are struggling in the world.
